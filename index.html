Technical Specs

Our product includes three components: data collection, data analysis, data presentation

Data Collection

Raspberry-pi in connection with external sensors used Python to collect data and then uploaded to MongoDB.

Data analysis

User's raw data is analyzed by algorithms in Microsoft Excel to evaluate the user's sleeping environment. JavaScript will run daily algorithms on servers.

Data Presentation

On the server's end, used node.js, express.js and MongoDB to build Restful APIs. On the client's end, used backbone.js and char.js to present the data to users. Used jQueryUI and bootstrap framework to design UI.

What is Unique about SNexus

More Valuable

By gathering data from our direct users and cooperation with hospitals and research institutions, we will have a large amount of first hand data. Then we will provide various type of data service based on the requirements from our customers.

More Reliable

Our device could track multiple factors, such as motion, sound, light, temperature and humidity, helping us have a better understanding of the person’s sleeping status in a more reliable and comprehensive way.

More Convenient

Our device has one fixed stand and one ring. The stand could track sound, light, temperature and humidity and the ring will track the sleeper’s motions. This combination is convenient enough to wear comparing with both the medical level appliance and the watch or band.
